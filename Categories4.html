Pilgrimage category

As one of the countries with a variety of religions, Indonesia also has multiple pilgrimage/religion
tourism spots spread all over Indonesia. Many people normally go to these places to perform ritual activities such as
remembrance, prayer, and others

1. Borobudur Temple (Budha)

This temple is located in Magelang, Central Java. It was built in the 9th century,
restored in 1911 and designated and 1991. Besides being the world’s largest Buddhist temple,
this temple is also a worship place for Buddhist people from Indonesia and other countries.

2. Prambanan Temple (Hindhu)

The Prambanan temple is the biggest Hindu temple in Indonesia with a complex area of
39.8 hectares, Besides being one of UNESCO’s world heritage, this temple site is also a
pilgrimage site for many Hindu people
