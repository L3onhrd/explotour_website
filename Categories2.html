Culture category

Indonesia is a country with hundreds of ethnicities. This creates a variety of cultures starting from
tradition, language, customs, and etc. There are many tourist spots that offer insight into
Indonesiaâ€™s culture. We are going to list some of them below

1. Naga village (Baduy culture)

Naga village is one of the villages located in Tasikmalaya, West Java.
This village maintains the culture of Baduy and advises from the ancestors
such as Tari topeng and Pencak silat

2. Ke'te Kesu

Toraja culture is the ancient culture that originated from South Sulawesi. One of the
villages in South Sulawesi that applies and maintains Toraja culture is the Ke'te Kesu
village. This village maintains several Toraja traditions such as the traditional burial
ceremony, Rambu Solo.
